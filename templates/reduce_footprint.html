How To Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

Conserve Electricity


Turn off the lights when you are not using them. Use LED lights instead of fluorescent and incandescent lights.

Avoid Excessive Food Consumption


Excessive food consumption can lead to food waste. Eat what you need and also avoid eating processed foods. Try to eat plant based and organic foods more often.

Travel Efficiently


Consider using a hybrid or electric car rather than gasoline cars. Use public transportation to get to work. Walk or bike to school or work if possible.

Avoid Plastics


Avoid buying plastic bottled waters and using plastic covers and wrappers. Using a reusable bag, spoons, and water bottles will you money and help the environment.
